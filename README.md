# My Blog
Welcome to my Blog - my corner. Here is where I share the articles I wrote and some articles in fields that I am interested in, like physics, cs, etc. I also decided to share my experiences in writing (as a junior researcher) and provide a small roadmap on how to improve your writing skills.  There is a quote section that is connected with api to provide a new random quote every time the page is refreshed, giving me and hopefully you as well a fresh dose of inspiration with every visit.
